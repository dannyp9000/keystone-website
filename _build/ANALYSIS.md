# Keystone OS — Site Architecture Analysis

## Product Identity
- **Name:** Keystone OS
- **Tagline:** The Operating System for Roofing Contractors
- **One-sentence:** AI-powered, all-in-one SaaS platform that manages the entire roofing business from lead capture through warranty service.
- **Elevator pitch:** Keystone OS replaces your fragmented stack of CRM, estimating, scheduling, photo documentation, and accounting tools with a single platform built exclusively for roofing contractors. Powered by Knox AI, it automates estimates, manages crews in the field, captures e-signatures on contracts, and gives you real-time visibility into every job, dollar, and crew member across all your locations.

## Audience
- **Primary:** Roofing company owners, office managers, and operations leads (commercial & residential)
- **Secondary:** Sales reps, production managers, field inspectors, crew leads
- **Pain points:** Too many disconnected tools, no real-time field visibility, manual estimating, paper contracts, lost photos, no AI automation, scaling multi-location is chaotic
- **Language:** Direct, professional, action-oriented. These are business owners who value efficiency and ROI.

## Core Value Props (Ranked)
1. **All-in-one platform** — Replace 5–8 separate tools with one system
2. **Knox AI Assistant** — 32 slash commands, natural language job management, photo analysis
3. **Complete revenue cycle** — Lead → Estimate → Contract → Invoice → Payment in one flow
4. **Field-ready portals** — Crew and Sales portals with GPS time tracking, mobile-first
5. **Multi-location scale** — HQ portal for franchise/multi-office oversight

## Competitive Edge
- Knox AI (no competitor has an embedded AI assistant)
- Multi-portal architecture (crew, sales, HQ, admin — purpose-built per role)
- CompanyCam replacement built-in (Key Cam)
- EagleView + Conklin deep integrations
- 310 RLS policies = enterprise-grade security from day one
